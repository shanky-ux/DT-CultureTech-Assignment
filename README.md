# Research Approach

The assignment was approached using a structured:

## Question → Hypothesis → Experiment

framework with emphasis on:
- Evidence-based validation
- Human QA review
- AI hallucination control
- Negative filtering
- Cross-verification from multiple sources
- Quality over quantity

---

# Validation & Guardrails

To reduce hallucination risk and improve research quality:
- Company claims were cross-verified using public sources
- Revenue estimates were validated where possible
- Manufacturing evidence was manually reviewed
- Borderline companies were manually re-evaluated
- Negative prompting and exclusion filters were applied during AI-assisted research
